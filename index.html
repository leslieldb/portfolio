Welcome!

Hi! I'm Prescott, an aspiring computer programmer/engineer, and welcome to my page!

I've been using computers ever since I was 3 years old, and I've loved everything about them. I learned my first programming language, HTML, when i was 8 years old, and I thought that was one of the most fascinating things I had utilized at the time.

I built my first PC in early 2019, and I've kept building off of it ever since. I started programming again in early 2021 by learning Python. I am now done my second year of community college, with 20+ languages and tools under my belt, incuding HTML, JavaScript, SQL, C++, C#, Java, Kotlin, Linux, Bootstrap, React, and Figma. I'm very interested and excited to learn more about the field, and I can't wait to use what I've learned to make my mark on the world!

What have I been up to recently?

Over the past few months, myself and 9 others have been developing an application to game-ify urban development called "PikUp". Led by Laura MacNeil and Ian Christian, we have been working on a brand new app that consists of features including tool sharing, co-shopping, and incentivizing picking up garbage.

The team has managed to build a mostly functional prototype of PikUp, with the tool share and garbage cleanup features almost completely finished.

With the semester coming to an end soon, development on this project may slow down, but development will still be continuing into the summertime. The team has done an outstanding job so far, and we look forward to releasing this to the public!


Stay tuned to be the first to be updated on this project!
