Managing Your Career as an Introvert - PMI

As an introvert with autism, I have a lot of trouble networking and finding ways to move in the workforce. This course helped me gain some new strategies, such as hoarding my downtime, and listening to myself to know when my body is telling me to take a break from social interactions.

Creating Your Personal Brand - LinkedIn

The first ever LinkedIn course I took, and still one of my favorites.
As a content creator, I have experience building a brand for my online community, but I didn't have that much experience regarding building my professional brand. This course gave me lots of new insights, such as showing activity frequently, asking for proper feedback, and building your brand advocates.
